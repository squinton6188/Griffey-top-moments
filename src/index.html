Top 10 Ken Griffey Jr. moments in Seattle

For 11 seasons from 1989-1999, and then another in 2009 (we’ll just pretend 2010 never happened), Marines fans could consider themselves lucky that they had a daily chance at seeing Ken Griffey Jr. do something incredible. Griffey was one of those rare players of whom you never wanted to miss an at-bat, or an inning in the field, at the risk of failing to see the play that everyone would be talking about the next morning. And as Griffey is set to be inducted into the Mariners’ Hall of Fame Saturday night, here is one man’s view of his top 10 Seattle moments, in ascending order.
